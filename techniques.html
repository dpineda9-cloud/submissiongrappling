No-Gi Technique Library

Explore our curated list of high-percentage No-Gi submissions. Visual learning is key to mastering these complex movements.

1. Rear Naked Choke (RNC)

Type: Blood Choke
The king of submissions. Constricts the carotid arteries from back control.

2. Triangle Choke

Type: Blood Choke
Uses your legs to strangle the opponent by trapping their arm and neck.

3. Guillotine Choke

Type: Air/Blood Choke
A powerful front headlock attack effective when an opponent shoots for a takedown.
