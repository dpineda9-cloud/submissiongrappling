No-Gi Technique Library

Explore our curated list of high-percentage No-Gi submissions. Visual learning is key to mastering these complex movements without the kimono.

1. Rear Naked Choke (RNC)

Type: Blood Choke
The king of No-Gi submissions. Requires back control and works on any opponent size.

2. Triangle Choke

Type: Blood Choke
Without the collar to grip, posture control is vital. Uses your legs to strangle.

3. Guillotine Choke

Type: Air/Blood Choke
The "High Elbow" variation (Marcelotine) is arguably the strongest front headlock attack.

4. Arm Triangle

Type: Blood Choke
A staple of MMA and No-Gi. Best finished from the mount or side control.

5. D'Arce Choke

Type: Arm Triangle
Uses long arms to thread the needle under the opponent's armpit and neck.

6. North-South Choke

Type: Strangle
A sneaky finish from top position that uses gravity and chest pressure. Pure No-Gi.
