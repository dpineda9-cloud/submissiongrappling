Technique Library

Explore our curated list of high-percentage submissions. Visual learning is key to mastering these complex movements.

1. Rear Naked Choke (RNC)

Type: Blood Choke
The highest percentage finish in grappling history. Requires back control.

2. Triangle Choke

Type: Blood Choke
Uses the opponent's own shoulder and your legs to cut off circulation.

3. Guillotine Choke

Type: Air/Blood Choke
A powerful front headlock attack effective in both Gi and No-Gi.

4. Arm Triangle

Type: Blood Choke
Uses massive shoulder pressure. Often finished from mount or side control.

5. D'Arce Choke

Type: Blood Choke
A sneaky variation of the arm triangle using long arms to thread the needle.

6. Bow and Arrow

Type: Collar Choke (Gi)
Uses the lapel for leverage. Often cited as the most powerful Gi choke.
